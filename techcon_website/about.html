History

TechCon began in 2015 as a small meetup of passionate developers and tech enthusiasts eager to share knowledge and ideas. Originally hosted in a local co-working space, the event quickly gained momentum due to its inclusive atmosphere and focus on emerging technologies. Over the years, TechCon has evolved into a global conference attracting thousands of participants from around the world. With each edition, it has expanded to include keynote speeches from industry leaders, hands-on workshops, startup showcases, and cutting-edge tech exhibitions — becoming a hub for innovation, collaboration, and future-forward thinking.

Mission

TechCon 2024 is driven by the belief that technology should empower, connect, and inspire. The conference aims to foster innovation by bringing together developers, creatives, educators, and business leaders under one roof. Our core goals include promoting open collaboration, showcasing cutting-edge advancements, and nurturing the next generation of tech talent. We are committed to inclusivity, accessibility, and sustainability ensuring that the future of technology benefits everyone, everywhere.
